## Launch Phase (Months 0-12)

### Month 1: Stealth Launch
**Target Users**: 1,000 beta testers + options trading community influencers

**Strategy:**
- Soft launch to r/options community (250k members)
- Invite-only access with referral system
- Focus on power users and feedback collection
- No paid marketing

**Success Metrics:**
- 500 active weekly users
- 4.5+ app store rating
- <2% churn rate
- 90% feature completion rate

### Months 2-3: Community Launch
**Target Users**: 5,000 registered, 2,000 active

**Strategy:**
- Open registration with waitlist system

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Technical Risks
**Risk**: Real-time data performance issues
**Mitigation**: Multi-provider data redundancy, edge caching

**Risk**: ML model accuracy degradation
**Mitigation**: Continuous model retraining, A/B testing

**Risk**: Mobile app store rejections
**Mitigation**: Early compliance review, phased feature rollout

### Market Risks
**Risk**: Regulatory changes affecting options trading
**Mitigation**: Legal advisory board, regulatory compliance automation

**Risk**: Market crash reducing options trading volume
**Mitigation**: Focus on hedging and risk management features

**Risk**: Competitor response (feature copying)
**Mitigation**: Patent portfolio, continuous innovation

### Business Risks
**Risk**: Customer acquisition cost inflation
**Mitigation**: Diversified marketing channels, strong referral program

**Risk**: Key team member departure
**Mitigation**: Documentation, knowledge sharing, equity retention

**Risk**: Broker partnership changes
**Mitigation**: Multiple broker relationships, native trading capability
