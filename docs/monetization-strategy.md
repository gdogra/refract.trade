# Refract.trade - Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Model Overview

**Hybrid Subscription + Performance-Based Model**
Target: $50M ARR within 3 years with 100,000 active users

## Subscription Tiers

### Refract Basic - $0/month (Freemium)
**Purpose**: User acquisition and basic options education

**Features Included:**
- Basic options chain viewing
- Simple strategy builder (covered calls, cash-secured puts)
- Basic portfolio tracking (up to 10 positions)
- Educational content library
- Paper trading sandbox (limited scenarios)
- Community access (view-only)

**Limitations:**
- No AI-powered alerts or suggestions
- No advanced risk analysis
- No tax optimization features
- No real-time strategy performance benchmarking
- Basic customer support only

### Refract Pro - $49/month
**Target**: Active retail options traders
**Market Size**: ~500,000 potential users

**Features Included:**
- Full Dynamic Risk Visualization Engine
- AI-powered position management alerts
- Advanced strategy builder (all strategies)
- Real-time Greeks heat maps
- Basic tax optimization (wash sale alerts)
- Community insights and benchmarking
- Unlimited paper trading scenarios
- Priority customer support

**Value Proposition**: Save $500+ monthly through better trade timing and risk management

### Refract Elite - $149/month
**Target**: Serious traders and small fund managers
**Market Size**: ~50,000 potential users

**Features Included:**
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- Full Tax Optimization Suite with advanced reporting
- Advanced AI strategy recommendations
- Historical scenario replay and backtesting
- Custom risk parameters and alerts
- API access for automated trading
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- 1-on-1 strategy consultation (monthly)
- White-glove customer support

### Refract Institutional - Custom Pricing
**Target**: Hedge funds, family offices, RIAs
**Market Size**: ~2,000 potential clients

**Features Included:**
- Multi-user team management
- Custom risk models and compliance rules
- Dedicated infrastructure and support
- Advanced API rate limits
- Custom integrations with existing systems
- Regulatory reporting automation
- On-premise deployment options

## Performance-Based Revenue

### Success Fee Model (Elite+ Only)
- **Performance Fee**: 5% of profits above benchmark
- **Benchmark**: Risk-free rate + 3% annually
- **High Water Mark**: Only charge fees on new profits
- **Transparency**: Full performance attribution analysis

### Example Calculation:
```
User Portfolio: $100,000
Annual Return: 18%
Risk-free Rate: 5%
Benchmark: 8%

Profit Above Benchmark: $100,000 × (18% - 8%) = $10,000
Performance Fee: $10,000 × 5% = $500
```
